THE FRAMEWORK — The Prophecy Engine

Overview. ProphetSolAI unifies on-chain, off-chain, and cultural signals inside a single prediction engine to detect momentum before it becomes obvious. The output is a composite probability — the Prophecy Score — representing a token’s near-term ability to attract attention and capital.

Data Ingestion. The engine ingests blockchain activity (transfer graphs, DEX liquidity, contract calls, holder distribution, CEX flows) in minute-level snapshots. In parallel, cultural streams (X/Telegram/Discord, search trends, GitHub cadence, meme detection) are pulled and timestamp-normalized with robust imputation for gaps and outliers.

Feature Engineering. Signals are organized into four bundles: On-Chain Health (active-address elasticity, holder-age mix, AMM depth, MEV sensitivity), Whale Topology (cluster coordination, accumulation→distribution phase detection), Meme Velocity (propagation entropy, source authority, CLIP-like visual-text coherence, narrative half-life), and Trust & Credibility (dev speed, version quality, dependency risk, contract anomaly score). Each bundle yields derivative momentum and regime features.

Modeling. Temporal modules (state-space / temporal-fusion) blend with graph neural networks over wallet-transaction graphs and contrastive multimodal encoders for memes. The ensemble is stacked via Bayesian evidence weighting, adapting per-asset and per-regime. Objective is not raw price — it is early attention & liquidity attraction.

Prophecy Score. The 0–100 score ships with auxiliaries: MoP (momentum-onset probability), MVQ (memetic vector quality), and WTI (whale transfer intent). Calibration uses leakage-safe walk-forward backtests across regimes.

Risk Controls. Outlier dampening, regime-switch penalties, and news-shock guardrails protect against overconfidence. Bytecode similarity checks flag copy/ponzi patterns; upgradeability risk is surfaced. Low-confidence outputs include uncertainty bands.

Continuous Learning. Daily online fine-tuning pushes live outcomes into the backtest pool, monitors data/concept drift, and reward-shapes successful early warnings while penalizing false positives — keeping the Oracle adaptive to new cycles.

Interpretability. Every score ships with Top-5 feature attributions (“Score 78 because: whale net-inflow ↑, memetic coherence ↑, sufficient DEX depth, accelerating dev cadence, propagation > threshold”), enabling transparent, repeatable reasoning.
