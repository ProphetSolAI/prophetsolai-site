What is ProphetSolAI?

ProphetSolAI is not a dashboard. It is an interpretation engine for the Solana realm. We ingest on-chain signals, liquidity tides, whale clusters, memetic surges, and sentiment currents — then translate them into human prophecy. Data becomes narrative. Metrics become myth. The aim is clarity: a confident, readable judgment about where attention and energy are moving, and why the culture might follow.

We do not promise certainty. We surface coherent possibilities — framed by trust, momentum, and community energy. The blockchain speaks. ProphetSolAI interprets.
